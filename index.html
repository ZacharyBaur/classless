Voyage to the Neon Nebula

A chrome-clad odyssey of the 23rd star-lane

In the year of the atomic sunset, the starship Aurora cuts a gilt arc across the silent cosmos — radar hum, pulsing consoles, and hearts of men made small beneath infinite starlight.

Mission Briefing

The Aurora's mission: chart the Neon Nebula and recover the lost signal of the Chrono-Beacon. Beware the whispering ion storms and the laugh of alien radio. Captain Juno issues the orders in a voice like polished brass.

  • Course: Andromeda Outrider, vector 7-B
  • Primary objective: Locate and secure Chrono-Beacon
  • Secondary objective: Make first diplomatic contact with the Lumen Folk
  1. Power up the main coils
  2. Calibrate the resonance dish
  3. Engage the flux drive at dawn
